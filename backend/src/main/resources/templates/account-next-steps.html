Thank you so much for requesting an account to SimpleReport: our free application for COVID-19 test result reporting! We have a few different links to share to keep things moving.

STEP 1: Administrator Identity Verification
The administrator for your site will need to go through an identity verification session with HHS. You should expect to receive an email to schedule your session with the subject: "Action Required: Final Step - Identity Proofing for SimpleReport". In some cases, this email may go to your spam folder, so if you do not see it, please check there as well. We recommend using your Driver's License and work ID as your two forms of identification for your ID session. After your account has been created you will be able to create accounts for additional users within your SimpleReport account.

STEP 2: SimpleReport Training
Training lasts about 30 minutes and is available as an online training or as a live session. If you'd like to schedule a live video session, please reach out to us at support@simplereport.gov. You can also practice on your own at training.simplereport.gov. This training site is designed to allow you to practice to get comfortable using SimpleReport before you start logging in through your actual account and using real data.

SimpleReport Introduction Video, Onboarding Guide, and Other Resources
